About the Board

A board built for cross-disciplinary stewardship.

The Biosphere 2 Board brings together leaders from industry, academia, philanthropy, and education. Their role is not to manage day-to-day operations — that is the job of the leadership directory — but to ensure the facility remains financially sustainable, scientifically credible, and mission-aligned for the decades ahead.
