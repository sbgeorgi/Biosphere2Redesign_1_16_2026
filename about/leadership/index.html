Directory

A leadership team built for integration.

Biosphere 2 is unusual not only as a facility but as an organization: research directors for each major biome sit alongside operations, innovation, and analytical leadership in a single flat structure. That integration is what makes cross-cutting experiments — like co-located food-and-energy studies or closed-loop life support trials — possible at this scale.

Governance

Leadership reports to a dedicated board.

The Biosphere 2 Board — chaired by Sarah Smallhouse, President of the Thomas R. Brown Foundations — sets strategic direction, approves major research partnerships, and stewards the facility's long-term mission. A separate Science Advisory Board, chaired by Deborah Goldberg of the University of Michigan, oversees research integrity.
