Conference Center Overview

Biosphere 2 Conference & Retreat Center offers a wide variety of spaces for conferences, workshops, retreats, and private events.

Seeking a dynamic venue for your next meeting or event? Nestled on 40 acres in the beautiful Santa Catalina Mountain Foothills, Biosphere 2 Conference & Retreat Center offers a non-traditional yet accessible venue with a variety of functional space and inspiring settings for productive conference, workshops, business meetings as well as corporate and private events.

The facilities at the Biosphere 2 Conference & Retreat Center comprise a campus village with 28 rustic Santa Fe-style houses holding 106 double-capacity sleeping rooms. Complete with in-suite kitchens, living areas, and internet access, the 3-5 bedroom casitas provide the perfect place to unwind and socialize in-between or after meetings. Sensible facility pricing of overnight accommodations ensures the Biosphere 2 Conference & Retreat Center is an affordable venue for your events and meetings.
