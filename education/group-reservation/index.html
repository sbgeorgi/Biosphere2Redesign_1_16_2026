What's Included

Every group visit comes with.

01

The Biosphere 2 Experience

A self-guided, app-driven audio tour through the human habitat, wilderness biomes, and behind-the-scenes research areas. Approximately 75 minutes, one mile of walking, and roughly 100 stairs. Curriculum-aligned for K-12; rich enough for university field trips.

02

Discounted admission

Groups of 15 or more receive a per-head discount off general admission. Title I Arizona schools may qualify for additional support. Final pricing is confirmed by your reservation coordinator after you submit the form below.

03

A dedicated coordinator

From first contact to the day-of-visit logistics, you'll work with one person who knows your group, your curriculum goals, and the calendar. No phone trees, no handoffs — just one name, one email, one phone number.

Before You Submit

What to know before booking.

A few things to have ready before you start the form — having them in hand will make the reservation process quick and painless.

  • Estimated group size (students + chaperones)
  • Two preferred dates, in case your first choice is taken
  • Grade level or age range of attendees
  • Curriculum focus — biomes, Earth science, climate, ecology
  • Accessibility needs within your group
  • School or organization billing information
  • Primary contact name, email, and phone

For Group Organizers

Frequently asked questions.

Groups of 15 or more receive discounted admission and dedicated coordinator support. Smaller groups are welcome to visit during regular public hours — just buy general-admission tickets online. There's no minimum for school groups seeking the K-12 app-guided experience, but the 15-person discount kicks in at 15.

We require at least two weeks' notice for group reservations. Spring (March–May) is our busiest season for K-12 field trips — book six to eight weeks ahead if you want a specific date in that window. Summer and fall are typically more flexible.

Yes. The Biosphere 2 Experience (self-guided app tour) is included with every admission. Guided add-ons — the Guided Lung Tour (30 min, behind-the-scenes pressure-balancing systems), the Library & History Tour (45 min, original Biospherian apartments), and the Birding Nature Walk (60 min, outdoor) — can be added to group visits subject to availability. Mention your interest in the form above and your coordinator will quote pricing and confirm availability.

Yes — Title I schools in Arizona may qualify for additional support toward admission and bus transportation. Indicate your Title I status in the "accessibility, timing, or curriculum notes" field on the form and your coordinator will reach out with options. Funding is limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis each academic year.

Group reservations may be rescheduled at no charge if requested at least 48 hours in advance. Cancellations within 48 hours of the scheduled visit may be subject to a processing fee. In the event Biosphere 2 closes due to severe weather or other unforeseen circumstances, all group reservations will be rescheduled or refunded in full.

The Biosphere 2 Cafe offers light refreshments, snacks, and beverages during operating hours, and there are outdoor patio areas where school groups can eat packed lunches. Food and drinks are not permitted inside the biomes. Many school groups leave lunches on the bus and eat at the patio between tour segments.

Absolutely. University classes — especially in environmental science, ecology, geology, hydrology, atmospheric science, and engineering — can arrange research-focused visits that go beyond the public tour. Select "research-focused (university)" on the visit-type field above, and your coordinator will work with you to identify a Biosphere 2 researcher who can host your class. We recommend booking four to six weeks ahead for research-focused visits.

Email bio2-info@arizona.edu or call (520) 838-6200. Our visitor services team is ready to help with questions about pricing, accessibility, tour content, or logistics — and to advise which visit type will work best for your group before you submit the form.
