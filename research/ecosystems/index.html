Integrated Science

Earth Systems Under One Roof

What makes Biosphere 2 truly unique is the physical proximity of five distinct biomes under a single glass envelope. Researchers can study how atmospheric, hydrological, and biogeochemical processes interact across ecosystem boundaries — tracing water as it evaporates from the ocean, falls as rain in the rainforest, percolates through the savanna, and evaporates from the desert.

This integrated Earth systems perspective is impossible to replicate in any other facility on Earth. It is the defining scientific value of Biosphere 2.
