Systems Data

The data acquisition server allows real-time monitoring of all data streams, whereas the two functions of the data server are to archive data and enable visualization and publishing.

Biosphere 2 was intended to be a tool for the scientific community. Researchers that have interests in performing experiments at the Biosphere 2 or use collected datasets are encouraged to contact Biosphere 2 affiliates to discuss potential collaborative opportunities.

LEO Data

The Landscape Evolution Observatory (LEO) sensor description, computations and additional datasets are available from the Biosphere 2 Research Wiki. Available datasets and live data streams include:

  • LEO EAST SLOPE DATA
  • LEO Center Slope Data
  • LEO WEST SLOPE DATA
  • LEO SUBSURFACE AREAL DATA
  • LEO MINI SLOPE DATA
  • LEO GENERAL DATA
  • LEO Water Chemistry Data
