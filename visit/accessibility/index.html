Accessibility

Planning Your Visit

Biosphere 2 welcomes visitors of all abilities. We strive to provide an inclusive experience while being transparent about the physical realities of our facility—a multi-level, glass-enclosed research structure built in the early 1990s.

The Biosphere 2 Experience is a self-guided, app-based audio tour that covers approximately 1 mile of walking, including both indoor and outdoor areas. The route includes approximately 100 stairs and passes through warm, humid biomes. While some areas of the facility have accessibility limitations due to its unique architecture, we offer alternative viewing options and accommodations wherever possible.

We encourage visitors with accessibility needs to contact us before your visit so we can help plan the best possible experience. Our staff is trained to assist and can provide detailed information about specific areas of concern.

Facility Details

What to Expect

Mobility & Wheelchair Access

The visitor center, cafe, and gift shop are fully wheelchair accessible. The initial exterior portions of the Biosphere 2 Experience are accessible by wheelchair.

  • Wheelchair-accessible restrooms available
  • Accessible parking spaces near the entrance
  • Ramps available at the visitor center and cafe
  • Interior biomes involve stairs—no elevator access
  • Alternative exterior routes available for some areas
  • Strollers not permitted in biome interior areas

Service Animals

Service animals are welcome throughout Biosphere 2 in accordance with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA). Please note the following:

  • Service animals must be harnessed and under control
  • Emotional support animals are not permitted inside biomes
  • Water bowls available—ask staff for assistance
  • Be mindful of warm, humid conditions in biomes
  • Please clean up after your service animal

Sensory Considerations

Biosphere 2 offers a rich sensory experience. Here's what to expect for visitors with sensory sensitivities:

  • Biomes are warm and humid (70–90°F, high humidity)
  • Some areas have limited natural light
  • The ocean biome includes wave sounds and splashing
  • Audio tour can be adjusted—use your own earbuds
  • Quieter times: weekday mornings, before 11 AM
  • Designated quiet spaces available—ask staff

App & Technology

The Biosphere 2 Experience app is designed with accessibility in mind:

  • Audio narration for all tour stops
  • Text transcripts available for audio content
  • Adjustable text size in the app
  • Compatible with iOS and Android screen readers
  • Loaner devices available—request in advance
  • App works offline after initial download

We're Here to Help

Request Accommodations

Our team is dedicated to ensuring every visitor can enjoy Biosphere 2.

If you have specific accessibility needs or questions about the facility, we encourage you to reach out before your visit. Our staff can provide detailed information, arrange accommodations, and help you plan the best route through the facility based on your needs.

Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance so we can best prepare for your visit. We're happy to discuss options for:

  • Wheelchair route planning
  • Loaner device requests
  • Sensory-friendly timing
  • Service animal accommodation
  • Alternative tour options
  • Group accessibility support
