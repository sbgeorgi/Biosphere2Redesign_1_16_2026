Your Personal Guide

Science Stories in the Palm of Your Hand

The Biosphere 2 Experience app transforms your visit into an immersive, self-guided journey. Through science stories and interviews with the researchers who work here, you'll have the opportunity to learn more about our amazing, world-class research as you traverse the exterior and through the human habitat and wilderness areas of Biosphere 2.

The app includes never-before-seen photos and videos that visualize the science and 30-year history of this extraordinary facility. It's like having a personal scientist guiding you through every biome.

App Features

What's Inside the App

The app is included free with your admission and enhances every step of your journey through Biosphere 2.

Exclusive Photos

Access never-before-seen photographs from Biosphere 2's 30-year history, including images from the original closed-system missions and behind-the-scenes research.

Video Stories

Watch exclusive video content that visualizes the science happening inside Biosphere 2, from rainforest research to ocean reef experiments.

Researcher Interviews

Hear directly from the scientists who work at Biosphere 2 as they share their research, discoveries, and personal stories from inside the facility.

Interactive Map

Navigate the facility with ease using the app's interactive map, which guides you through each biome and highlights key points of interest.

Science Stories

Learn about cutting-edge research on climate change, water cycles, and ecosystem responses through engaging, accessible science narratives.

Audio Narration

Listen to immersive audio narration at each stop. Bring your earbuds or headphones for the best experience as you walk through the biomes.

How It Works

Four Simple Steps

Download

Get the free Biosphere 2 Experience app from the App Store or Google Play before you arrive. Cell service at the facility can be limited.

Arrive & Check In

Purchase tickets online or at the gate. Show your ticket at the entrance and get oriented at the visitor center before starting your tour.

Open & Explore

Open the app, plug in your earbuds, and follow the interactive map. The app guides you through exterior areas, the human habitat, and wilderness biomes.

Learn & Discover

At each stop, listen to stories, watch videos, and explore photos that bring the science and history of Biosphere 2 to life. Go at your own pace!

What You'll See

Journey Through Earth's Biomes

The app-guided tour takes you through multiple distinct ecosystems, each with its own research story. From the steamy tropical rainforest to the wave-driven ocean reef, from the desert to the savanna—you'll experience the full diversity of Earth's living systems under one roof.

Along the way, you'll also explore the human habitat where Biospherians lived during the 1990s missions and learn about the engineering marvels that keep Biosphere 2 running.
